Section 03
Privacy
Saved to your profile and enforced across matching and messaging.
Discretion mode
When you open someone's profile, that visit is not recorded — you won't show up in their profile-view history.
Block colleagues
If you share the same industry on your profile, you and that member won't appear in each other's match queue or profile views. Applies when either of you has this on. Already-connected members are unaffected.
Mutual-only visibility
Members who aren't connected with you see only basics — name, photo, role, and location. Your full bio, values, and lifestyle details unlock after a mutual connection.
Read receipts
When on, people you message can see when you've read their messages. Turn off to hide your read status from them.
